SoundQR

Your voice, encoded in QR.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026

SoundQR is a fully offline app. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with external servers, third parties, or anyone else.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

SoundQR does not collect, store remotely, or transmit:

  • Voice recordings or audio data
  • QR codes or encoded payloads
  • Encryption passwords
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

2. Permissions and How They Are Used

Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)
Used solely to record your voice within the app. Audio is processed entirely on your device using the Codec2 audio codec and is never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

Camera (CAMERA)
Used solely to scan QR codes through the in-app camera viewfinder. Camera frames are processed on-device in real time and are never saved or transmitted.

Storage (WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — Android 8 and below only)
Used to save QR code images to your device's storage when you choose to save them. No files are uploaded or shared without your explicit action.

3. On-Device Storage

SoundQR stores a local history of your encoded QR sessions in a database on your device. This includes timestamps, recording durations, codec settings, and QR thumbnail images. This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time from within the app.

4. Encryption

SoundQR offers optional AES-256-GCM encryption for QR payloads. If you choose to encrypt, a password you provide is used to derive an encryption key on your device using PBKDF2. The password is never stored, logged, or transmitted — it exists only in memory for the duration of the operation.

5. Third-Party Services

SoundQR does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or cloud services. The app functions entirely without an internet connection.

6. Children's Privacy

SoundQR does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected at all, the app poses no privacy risk to users of any age.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
